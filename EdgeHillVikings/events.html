Events for the Vikings

The EHU Vikings events will be going on from September's Freshers week until May time. Events for the team are Socials, Charity events, Camps and Recruitment. The events going on will be lister below with month and date aswell as what will happen. The Vikings Family aims to organize as many events as we can to support our team, community and well as having fun and bond together. We also like to organize events with other societies and teams as we all wear the same Edge Hill badge which unifies us as a whole.

  • 1st
    November

    EHU Vikings Movember

    Our beloved EHU Vikings will be part yet again of the Movember event and it will happen at 1pm in the Hub, our one and only Joe Sandle will be shaving his hair for the event and plenty of our players will be shaving their beards to raise funds for a the Movembr charity.

  • 5th
    November

    Lancaster Bombers VS Edge Hill Vikings

    Your Vikings will be going away to Lancaster to play against the Bombers for their first DIV 2 Season game.
